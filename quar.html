    My Quarantine

    Hello all! Due to COVID-19, I had more free time to explore activities during quarantine. I picked up baking/cooking, painting, and reading books - all of which I didn't have time to do during the school year.

    I made a lot chocolate scones using my cooking teacher's recipe (posted below)!


    Before all the stores closed down, I made sure to buy some paint and paper in case the lockdown was longer than expected (which it unfortunately was). I found my inspiration through the Instagram artist @boelterdesignco! I focused my paintings towards nature and landscape. I felt very relaxed while, and all of my attention was focused on these illustrations!
